Don't Repeat Yourself (DRY):

Every piece of knowledge should have a single, unambiguous, 
authoritative source of representation.

Single Source of Truth

We often think of duplication of code, but DRY is more than that.

Duplication of effort, not just duplication of code.

If we do manual testing, that is a violation of the DRY principle
If we do manual deployment, that is a violation of the DRY princple
